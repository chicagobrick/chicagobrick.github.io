Our History

We meet the highest building standards and satisfy our customers expectations. In 1987 Chicago Brick Work, Inc. was established on the basis of providing top quality masonry repair work. Starting off as a small two man crew the business has grown to being one of the leading masonry contractors in Chicagoland. We are based out of Chicago's Portage Park neighborhood. We repair and restore all types of stone, concrete, and brick. We are family owned and operated. No sub contractors are used on our job sites. The owner of the property is always kept up to date on the status of work, and the project timeline. Making sure the customer is satisfied is the top priority. We have been servicing brick on both residential and commercials properties for over 29 years, every aspect of our service involves the utmost professionalism and courtesy. We carry full Workman's Compensation Insurance as well as General Liability Insurance. Our employee's have decades of experience on the job.
